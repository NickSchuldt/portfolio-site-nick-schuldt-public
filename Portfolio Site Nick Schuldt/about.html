About Me

My Story

I got into UX design after looking into what I could do with a psychology degree after I earned mine. Unfortunately at the time I didn't do enough research into the field and thought it wasn't for me. fast forward a few year I decided I wanted to change careers and I stumbled back to UX design. This time I would not make the same mistake. After doing lots of research into the field watching videos on UX design and really starting to understand it I decided I was going to take the UX design course through CareerFoundry

In the meantime I continued to work in a high volume food court where I would go from managing just 20-30 people to end up assisting in supervising over 350 employees. In this role I would also take on the responsibility of ordering, scheduling, and delegating responsibilities. I would also be responsible for communicating with higher ups and assist in make sure those I supervise were on board and working towards the overall goals and vision of the company.
